A NOTE FROM THE CHEF

DON'T GET ME WRONG: I love the restaurant business. I'm simply not going to deceive anybody about the life as I've seen it. For me, the cooking life has been a long love affair, with moments both sublime and ridiculous.

FIRST COURSE

FOOD IS GOOD

MY FIRST INDICATION THAT food was something other than a substance one stuffed in one's face when hungry came after fourth-grade elementary school. It was on a family vacation to Europe, on the Queen Mary. I asked our patient British waiter what this delightfully cool, tasty liquid was. 'Vichyssoise,' came the reply.

FOOD IS SEX

IN 1973, UNHAPPILY IN love, I graduated high school a year early. I spent most of my waking hours drinking, smoking pot, and doing my best to amuse, outrage, and impress. While her new groom and family chawed happily, here was the blushing bride, getting an impromptu send-off from a total stranger. And I knew then: I wanted to be a chef.

FOOD IS PAIN

I DON'T WANT YOU to think that everything up to this point was about fornication. There was, in 1974, no culinary culture that I was aware of. Our few culinary heroes were admired more for their studliness on the line - meaning number of dinners served and amount of pain and heat endured.

INSIDE THE CIA

BURNING WITH A DESIRE for vengeance, I applied myself to gaining entry to the Culinary Institute of America. CIA was a bit of a departure. I was an enrolled student where everyone wore identical white uniforms and funny paper hats.

THE RETURN OF MAL CARNE

MY TRIUMPHANT RETURN TO Provincetown - halfway through the program at CIA - came the following summer. I was determined to outwork, outlast and in every way impress my old tormentors.

SECOND COURSE

WHO COOKS?

WHO'S COOKING YOUR FOOD anyway? If the chef is anything like me, the cooks are a dysfunctional, mercenary lot, fringe-dwellers motivated by money and a grim pride. Line cooking is more about consistency, about mindless, unvarying repetition.

FROM OUR KITCHEN TO YOUR TABLE

I SAW A SIGN advertising 'Discount Sushi'. I can't imagine a better example of Things To Be Wary Of. Good food and good eating are about risk. I never order fish on Monday, unless I'm eating at Le Bernardin.
